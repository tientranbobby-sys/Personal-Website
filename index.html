Doraemon

MS-903 "Doraemon", or simply Doraemon or Doraemon Nobi (ドラえもん Doraemon) is the titular deuteragonist of the Doraemon franchise. After being manufactured in a Factory in the 22nd century, he is sent back in time by his owner, Sewashi Nobi, to help his great-great-grandfather, Nobita Nobi, become a successful person and have a better future. Doraemon loves Dorayaki, plays with the other cats, and he has magical tools from the future that grants skills and ability that normal people won't be able to do.

Doraemon being from the future allows him to be able to store many useful, unique gadgets in his magical pouch like the Anywhere Door, Air Canon, Gulliver Tunnel, and Dress Up Camera. These are just some of his popular gadget as he has over 1 000 of them!

The Anywhere Door (Dokodemo Door) is an iconic, pink teleportation gadget from the Doraemon anime/manga series, allowing users to instantly travel to any location by opening it. It operates by bypassing physical distance, with common constraints often limiting its reach to within 100,000 light-years

The Bamboo Copter (known as Take-copter in Japanese and The Hopter in some dubs) is one of Doraemon's most iconic 22nd-century gadgets. It is a small, yellow propeller-driven device with a suction cup that is placed on the head, allowing users to fly freely

The Big Light is a iconic flashlight-shaped gadget from Doraemon that enlarges objects or people when shined upon them. Often paired with the Small Light (which shrinks items), it is used to increase the size of items, such as toys, food, or characters, temporarily or permanently

The Translation Gummy (known in Japanese as Honyaku Konnyaku or Translation Konjac) is a secret gadget from the Doraemon series. It is a magical, edible jelly that, when consumed, allows the user to instantly understand and speak any language in the universe, including alien languages and animal communication
