Doraemon

MS-903 "Doraemon", or simply Doraemon, is the titular character of the Doraemon franchise. Manufactured in the 22nd century, he was sent back in time by Sewashi Nobi to help Nobita Nobi become successful and create a better future.

Doraemon loves Dorayaki, enjoys playing with other cats, and owns many magical gadgets from the future that grant amazing powers and abilities.

Doraemon has over 1,000 useful gadgets stored in his magical pouch.

The Anywhere Door lets users instantly travel anywhere.

The Bamboo Copter allows people to fly freely.

The Big Light enlarges objects or people.

The Translation Gummy lets users understand any language.
